Privacy & Cookie Policy

Placeholder policy · Last updated July 20, 2026

Draft for legal review. This generic policy cannot guarantee compliance with every applicable federal or state privacy law and should be reviewed before public launch.

1. Information collected

The service collects the username, email address, and authentication information needed to create and operate an account. Supabase processes account passwords; Websites with Whimsy does not display them.

The service also stores board names, introductions, Post-It text, links, settings, Archive state, uploaded images and videos, media descriptions, and related technical metadata you choose to provide.

2. How information is used

Information is used to authenticate users, save and restore boards, store uploaded media, maintain account security, provide requested features, troubleshoot errors, and enforce the Terms of Use.

A board preview is used publicly or promotionally only when the user affirmatively submits it to the Gallery and accepts the Gallery license.

3. Service providers

Supabase provides authentication, database, and media storage services. Cloudflare provides website hosting and delivery. These providers process technical and account data as necessary to provide their services and under their own applicable terms and privacy practices.

4. Browser storage, cookies, and tracking

The current service uses necessary browser storage to maintain authentication sessions, remember application state, and offer migration of an older locally saved board. This storage is needed for requested account and board functionality.

Websites with Whimsy does not currently include advertising cookies, behavioral advertising, or analytics trackers. If nonessential analytics, advertising, or tracking technology is introduced, this policy and any required consent or opt-out controls must be updated before that technology is enabled.

Your browser can remove local storage and cookies, but doing so may sign you out or remove an unimported local board.

5. Retention and deletion

Account and board information is retained while needed to provide the service. Users can delete individual boards through the application. Deleted media and board records are scheduled for removal from the associated storage systems. Backup or operational copies may persist for a limited period.

An account-deletion request process and retention schedule must be finalized before public launch.

6. Public content

Gallery previews are public. Do not include private or identifying information that you do not want others to see. Public previews may be copied or shared by others outside the service’s control.

7. Children’s privacy

The service is not directed to children under 13. The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act may impose notice, consent, deletion, and other requirements if the service knowingly collects personal information from a child under 13.

8. Security and choices

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored data, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security. Users should use a unique password and protect access to their email account.

Formal privacy contact information, state-specific rights procedures, and an account-deletion process must be added before public launch.
